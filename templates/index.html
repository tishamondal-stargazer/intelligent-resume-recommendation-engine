Frequently Asked Questions

What is resume matching?
Resume matching uses AI to compare job descriptions with resumes and find the best candidates based on skills, experience, and qualifications.
How does AI calculate match scores?
We use TF-IDF vectorization + Cosine Similarity to convert text to numbers and calculate similarity percentages.
What is ATS score?
ATS score measures how well your resume will perform with Applicant Tracking Systems used by companies.
Is my data secure?
Yes! Your data is processed in real-time and never stored on our servers.
